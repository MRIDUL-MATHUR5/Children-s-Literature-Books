Who We Are

Celebrating the Art of Children's Literature

The Association of Writers and Illustrators for Children (AWIC) is India's foremost body dedicated to promoting quality literature and illustration for young readers. Since 1975, we have championed storytellers, poets, and artists whose work enriches childhood.


Through our journals, events, and workshops, AWIC fosters a community where creativity and education meet — nurturing the next generation of readers and creators.
