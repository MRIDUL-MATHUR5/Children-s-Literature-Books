The Creator

Hi, I'm Mridul Mathur, a fourth-year B.Tech student at IIIT Nagpur with a passion for technology, design, and building meaningful digital experiences.

I built this website to organise and present AWIC's rich archive of children's literature in a clean, accessible way — making decades of research and writing easily available to readers, educators, and scholars.

I enjoy combining data, design, and functionality to create platforms that are not just useful, but intuitive and engaging. My goal is always to build things that solve real problems and deliver a seamless experience.

If you have an idea you'd like to bring to life or are looking to build a website, feel free to reach out — I'd love to collaborate.
